# Code of conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project for everyone.

Contributors are expected to communicate constructively, critique ideas rather
than people, respect privacy, accept responsibility for mistakes, and prioritize
the safety of users whose screen content may be processed by Longview.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate exposure of
private information, or sustained disruption are not acceptable in project
spaces or when representing the project.

Report conduct issues privately to the maintainers through a GitHub security
advisory. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation,
or ban participants when necessary to protect the community.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
